Ethiverse

Tech Solutions by the People, for the People, of the People

Our Mission

To create transparent and ethical tech solutions that empower users with control over their data and experiences.

The Problem

  • Current tech solutions controlled by investors
  • Users lack control over features and data
  • Quality degradation due to profit-driven motives

Our Solution

  • Open-source, transparent solutions
  • User-owned data and experiences
  • Ethical, community-driven development

Key Features

  • Secure, user-controlled data storage
  • AI-powered solutions with user data integration
  • NFT-based ownership tracking
  • Transparent governance through DAO concepts

Target Audience

  • Everyday consumers seeking ethical tech solutions
  • Builders and developers interested in open-source projects
  • Supporters of ethical technology

Business Model

  • Subscription-based with pay-per-use model
  • Token system for accessing various functionalities
  • Development costs shared among users

Our Advantage

  • Accessible open-source solutions for non-technical users
  • Integration of decentralized technologies with user-friendly interfaces
  • AI-driven language and education gap bridging

Current Status

Prototypes and proof of concepts developed

Moving towards MVP (Minimum Viable Product)

Team and Partnerships

  • Collaboration with Code Blossom for education
  • Partnership with Hazgeek for regulatory support
  • Seeking advisors and early adopters

Roadmap

Short-term: Make AI accessible globally

Long-term: Create an ecosystem of transparent, ethical tech solutions

Challenges and Solutions

  • Measuring contributions in collaborative projects
  • Balancing privacy with functionality
  • Developing a fair governance framework
